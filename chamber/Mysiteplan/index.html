OMR SaaS Company - Site Plan

Site Name

OMR SaaS Company - The name represents my upcoming software company, offering Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) solutions for various industries.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a landing page to present my OMR SaaS company. It will showcase our product, features, and services while allowing potential customers to contact us.

Site Structure

  • Home
  • About Us
  • Product
  • Features
  • Contact Us

Scenarios

  • How does OMR technology improve data collection? For your music transcription software, OMR technology improves data collection by automating the recognition of sheet music, ensuring quick and accurate transcription. It reduces manual errors, detects key and time signatures, and supports bulk processing for choir and ensemble music. Additionally, it enables tonic solfa conversion, multi-instrument recognition, and real-time editing, streamlining music digitization for composers and choristers.
  • What features does the OMR SaaS product offer? Your OMR-based music transcription software will offer sheet music scanning, tonic solfa transcription, key & time signature detection, multi-instrument recognition, MIDI/audio export, real-time editing, batch processing for choirs, and AI-powered note correction. This ensures accurate, efficient, and user-friendly music digitization.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #004AAD (Used for headings, buttons, and key sections)
  • Secondary Color: #F4F4F4 (Used for backgrounds and contrast areas)

Typography

  • Heading Font: Poppins (Bold, modern look for headings)
  • Body Font: Open Sans (Clean and readable for general content)

Wireframe
